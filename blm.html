Black Lives Matter

What is Black Lives Matter?

Black Lives Matter is a movement started in response to the acquittal of George Zimmerman in the shooting of African-American teen Trayvon Martin. All throughout history, Black people have been supressed and treated as less than equal. THAT IS NOT OK. Black lives are absolutely lives and they deserve to be treated as such. It is up to every one of us to be the force of change and to say that enough is enough! Black Americans are more than twice as likely than white Americans to be killed by excessive use of police force (31 fatal shootings per million for black Americans and 13 fatal shootings per million for white Americans). We urge you to be an agent of change and always remember to be empathetic towards others. Everyone out there is a human just like you!
